Networking

Networking is important for everything in our society, to connect with friends, collegues, family,
and networking is most vital for businesses to connect with their teams and clients. Without the
hardware necessary for networking a business is a lot more prone to failure when their competitors
most likely have the upper hand in spreading their influence with tools tailored towards networking
in their space of expertise.

Scenerio

The fictional scenerio that I have chosen to analyze is a small tech startup business with five employees.
The company was just formed and the team needs to budget for hardware and devices used for networking in
order to work together on their product and to reach out to make connections with the potential clients
and investors.

List of Hardware

  • Router - A high-performance router that provides a stable and secure connection for all employees within the local area network (LAN).
  • Computing Devices - Powerful laptops or desktop computers for each team member, complete with efficient processing power and ample storage to meet their computing demands.
  • Printer - A dependable printer to produce physical copies of important documents, invoices, and other office materials.
  • Network Attached Storage - A Network Attached Storage (NAS) device to store, manage and share vital data securely across the network.
  • Network Cables - Network cables to connect the different hardware components and ensure seamless communication between them.
  • Wi-Fi Access Points - Wi-Fi access points to furnish wireless internet connectivity for mobile devices and laptops.
  • Switches - A flexible switch to accommodate the growth of the network and connect multiple devices simultaneously.

Expenses

  • Router: $100-$300
  • Switch: $100-$200
  • PCs or Laptops: $600-$1500
  • Printer: $100-$400
  • Network Attached Storage: $200-$800
  • Wi-Fi Access Points: $50-$150
  • Network Cables: $20-$100

Based on the amount of five employees and depending on the specific models purchased, the total cost of all the hardware
will come to about $4000 to $8000. This is a rough estimate and not an exact total, again it depends on how much money the
startup is willing to invest in this equipment.
